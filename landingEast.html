East Seattle

Madison Park, Capitol Hill, Madrona and Montlake make up the East Seattle region. This expansive district contains a mix of tree-shaded streets flanked by some of Seattleâ€™s loveliest century-old mansions and commercial thoroughfares. Capitol Hill has been home to some of the city's most prominent locally owned coffeehouses. The neighborhood is considered a test market for coffee houses by Starbucks Corporation. It is also one of the city's most prominent nightlife and entertainment districts, and is the center of the city's counterculture communities. Numerous parks, the Arboretum, Madison Park Beach, Japanese Garden, Volunteer Park, Asian Art Museum are just a few of the attractions this area has to offer.
