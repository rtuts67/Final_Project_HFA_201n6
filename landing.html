North Seattle

North Seattle includes Northgate, Ravenna, Greenwood and many more options for you to choose a home. This includes plenty of shopping near U Village and NorthGate Mall along with many public libraries. School districts included are the North Cluster of the Seattle school district. You could go golfing at the Jackson Park Golf Club. You could pick one of the many parks and hit up the swing sets or grassy knolls.
